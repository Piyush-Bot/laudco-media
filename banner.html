Steps to Embed

    For Youtube Video
  • Go to www.youtube.com . Select and open your video that you want to embed.
  • Go to video share option and select Embed. It will generate the embed code.
  • Select the embed code, and copy it.
  • Open your HTML page in editor and paste it into the element where you want to display.
  • Save your HTML page.
  • We have now embedded content in our HTML page.
